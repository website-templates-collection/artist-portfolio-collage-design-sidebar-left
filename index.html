ABOUT THE ARTIST

I'm Elena Maria, a visual artist based in Barcelona, creating expressive works that explore the intersection of traditional techniques and contemporary themes.

My work is characterized by bold colors, textured surfaces, and a collage-like approach to composition. I draw inspiration from urban landscapes, nature, and the human experience.

With over 10 years of professional experience, my work has been exhibited internationally and featured in numerous publications.
